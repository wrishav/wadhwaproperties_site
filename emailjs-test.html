Use this tool to verify your EmailJS credentials are working correctly.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • 422 Error ("The recipients address is corrupted"): Go to your EmailJS Dashboard > Email Templates > Open your template. In the "To Email" field, remove {{to_email}} and type your email address (e.g., wadhwaproperties16@gmail.com) directly.
  • 400 Error: Usually means incorrect Public Key or Service ID.
  • 412 Error: Usually means the Public Key is disabled or incorrect.
